Coconut rice recipe

Little Story

Coconut rice recipe | nariyal chawal | south indian coconut an healthy and flavoured white rice recipe mainly cooked with grated fresh coconut and basmati rice.

he recipe is very similar to the other popular version of coconut milk rice but is prepared in a more simpler way. the dish typically consumed with a spicy curry like kurma or kuzhambu, but can also be consumed as it is for breakfast and lunch.coconut rice recipe | nariyal chawal | south indian coconut rice recipe perhaps one of the most flavoured rice recipe which is not only popular in south india, but also in south east asian countries.

Most commonly, coconut rice is mainly prepared with coconut milk in many regions. however for the sake of simplicity i have used grated coconut with cooked basmati rice.to be honest i am not a huge fan of this recipe and i personally do not like sweet or coconut flavoured rice. but having said that i prepare it quite often as my husband likes to relish it with either kurma or even coconut based sambar. but more popularly coconut rice is mainly eaten as it is or with kuzhambu recipes. whenever i plan to prepare this recipe, i make sure to prepare it in small quantities.

The idea is to have this flavoured rice as side dish and not as main dish. basically i prepare the coconut based curries with it, so that it can be used for both nariyal chawal and plain steamed rice. further it can also be a good breakfast option with your left over rice and can be enjoyed with simple chutney recipes.

Ingredients

3 tsp oil

1 tsp mustard / rai

½ tsp urad dal

1 tsp chana dal

-------------

few curry leaves

1 dried red chilli

2 green chilli (slit)

1 inch ginger (finely chopped)

---------

10 cashew / kaju

¾ cup coconut (grated)

2 cup cooked rice

½ tsp salt

Instructions

firstly, in a large kadai heat 3 tsp oil and saute 1 tsp mustard, ½ tsp urad dal, 1 tsp chana dal, few curry leaves, 1 dried red chilli.

also add 2 green chilli, 1-inch ginger. saute slightly.

------------

now add 10 cashews and roast till the cashews turn golden brown.

add in ¾ cup grated coconut and saute for a minute or till raw smell goes away.

further, add 2 cups cooked rice and ½ tsp salt. mix gently without breaking the rice.

------------

now cover and simmer for 2 minutes, or till rice absorbs coconut flavour.

finally, serve south indian style coconut rice with kuzhambu or any curry.
