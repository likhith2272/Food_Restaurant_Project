chilli garlic breadsticks recipe | chilli garlic toast sticks | garlic breadsticks a perfect and tasty evening snack recipe made with leftover bread slices with the topping of chilli & garlic seasoning. it is an ideal starter snack recipe which can be served just before your pizza or burger meal.

traditionally these sticks are made with specific bread in a high heat oven, but this recipe is made in a pan on top of the gas stove.chilli garlic breadsticks recipe | chilli garlic toast sticks | garlic breadsticks with video recipe. garlic-based snacks are hugely popular among indians and are made and served for myriad purposes. especially when the garlic toppings are combined crispy bread, it makes a flavoured garlic bread snack recipe.

one such popular and tasty garlic snack recipe is chilli garlic breadsticks recipe known for its combination of taste and garlic flavour.i am a huge fan of garlic bread recipes, and it is one of the must recipes whenever i have burger or pizza meal. however, lately, i have started liking more of these sticks rather than the traditional garlic bread loaf. it is not only easy and simple to make but also lite and turns out to be more crispy in texture.

these sticks can also be made in toaster or grill. but you may have to crisp it first and then apply the butter and garlic spread on top o it. hence i always prefer the tawa pan option. i cut these bread into the thin slice, apply the garlic butter and roast than in a slow flame. the flame has to be very low, otherwise, there is a high chance of getting burnt. also, the other tip is to apply the garlic butter to just one side. if you apply to it to both sides, it may turn soggy and may not have the same crunchy and crisp texture.

Ingredients

¼ cup butter (softened)

6 clove garlic (grated)

2 tbsp coriander (finely chopped)

-------------

1 tsp mixed herbs

1 tsp chilli flakes

¼ tsp white pepper powder

---------

¼ tsp salt

4 slice bread (white or brown)

Instructions

firstly, in a small bowl take ¼ cup butter and 6 clove garlic.

add 2 tbsp coriander, 1 tsp mixed herbs, 1 tsp chilli flakes ¼ tsp white pepper powder and ¼ tsp salt.

mix well making sure everything is well combined.

------------

then cut into thick strips.

now cut the sides of bread. i have cut only 2 sides of the bread.

spread the garlic butter on the bread stirps.

------------

toast the breadsticks on pan. you can alternatively bake in oven or grill.

turn over and toast until the bread turns golden brown.

finally, enjoy chilli garlic breadsticks with more chilli flakes.
