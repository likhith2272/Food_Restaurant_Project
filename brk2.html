Gobi Paratha Recipe

Little Story

gobi paratha recipe | gobi ka paratha | gobhi paratha recipe a popular stuffed indian paratha recipe which is stuffed with spiced cauliflower. cauliflower paratha is typically served with pickle and yoghurt which is ideal for breakfast and lunch boxes.

gobi paratha recipe | gobi ka paratha | gobhi paratha recipe basically a north indian or punjabi cuisine parantha recipe that is stuffed with flavoured grated cauliflower. the bread is prepared with whole wheat flour dough to which spiced grated gobi mixture is stuffed. moreover, there are several recipes for gobhi parantha which mainly differs in its stuffing. i have posted several paratha recipe till now but this simple gobi paratha recipe was long due. i think i overlooked to and i assumed that i had already posted this recipe.

moreover i started getting several requests for this exotic indian flat bread recipe. hence i thought to post this recipe quickly on my blog. in this recipe i have used only cauliflower stuffing, but this can be easily extended by adding onions, potatoes and even capsicum. perhaps i would share that recipe soon. some important tips and suggestion for a perfect and yummy gobhi ka paratha recipe.

firstly, the dough needs to be kneaded very smoothly so that the spiced cauliflower is easily stuffed. secondly, and more importantly, apply butter or ghee on paratha while frying after you have roasted the paratha once. this ensures the paratha to cook perfectly otherwise it will turn oily and half cooked. lastly, i have added red chilli powder to gobi stuffing, but additionally green chillies too can be added to make it more spicy.

Ingredients

1 tsp ginger / adrak (grated)

2 cup gobi / cauliflower (grated)

½ tsp turmeric / haldi

½ tsp kashmiri red chili powder / lal mirch powder<

-------------

tsp coriander powder / daniya powder

½ tsp cumin powder / jeera powder

½ tsp garam masala powder

½ tsp aamchur powder / dry mango powder

---------

salt to taste

2 tsp coriander

1 cup whole wheat flour / atta

water as required for kneading

¼ cup wheat flour for dusting

Instructions

firstly, heat oil and saute ginger.

further add grated cauliflower (gobi) and saute for 5 minutes.

furthermore add all spices and salt. saute well.

finally, add coriander leaves and keep aside.

------------

firstly, pinch a medium sized ball from chapathi dough, roll about 5 inches in diameter.

place the prepared stuffing in the center.

take the edge and start pleating bringing to center.

and press the pleats from center.

------------

furthermore, sprinkle some flour and roll to of a chapati size.

firstly, on a hot tava place the rolled paratha and cook both sides.

also add oil / ghee and press slightly.

finally, serve hot gobi parathas with sauce, raita or pickle.
